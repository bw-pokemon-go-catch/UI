Gotta Catch Worthy 'mon!

We help to achieve the dream of all Pokèmon trainers, every step of the way, one Pokèmon at a time. No math. No guesswork. With our Worthymon Catch Gauge you'll know what is and isn't worth catching 100% of the time!

The Gauge

Have you ever been alone in the woods at 3:00AM, your trusty session of Pokèmon GO at your side, when suddenly a mighty Pidgey springs forth from the grass? "What should I do?", you wondered. "Should I run?", you asked. "Should I attempt a catch, even though I only have two Pokèballs to my name?", you pondered. Maybe you decided to take a chance. Maybe you decided that discretion was the better part of valor. Either way, wouldn't it have been nice to know, beyond a shadow of a doubt, what the correct decision was? That's where Worthymon comes in to play!

Can I Catch That?

With Worthymon, we take the guesswork completely out of the equation so you can relax and have fun! You'll never again wonder whether or not you should catch a particular Pokèmon, even ones as scary as Gyrados.

Be The Hero!

Worthymon will help you to more accurately and efficiently hunt down the Pokèmon you'll need to do battle across the globe! Never again let that one wasted attempt prevent you from being the very best at a gym!

Waste Not, Want Not.

Why waste not only your time, but your precious resources? Did you know that the algorithms in Worthymon will take your items into account when calculating whether or not a Pokèmon is actually worth catching?! Waste no more!
